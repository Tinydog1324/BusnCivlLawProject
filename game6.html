How to Play Legal Terms Match

Game Objective

Match legal terms with their correct definitions to test your knowledge of business and civil law concepts.

Game Rules

  1. Click any card to flip it and reveal either a legal term or definition.
  2. Click a second card to find its matching pair.
  3. If the term matches its definition, both cards stay face up.
  4. If they don't match, both cards will flip face down.
  5. Remember the locations of cards to make matches more quickly.

Winning the Game

Match all pairs of terms and definitions to win! Try to complete the game with fewer attempts and in less time.

Tips

  • Pay attention to the cards you've already flipped.
  • Take your time to read and understand each definition.
  • Try to remember the location of similar terms.
