Contract Negotiation Simulator - Game Rules

Game Overview

You are a business negotiator tasked with securing favorable contract terms while maintaining positive business relationships. Navigate through complex negotiations by proposing terms, analyzing offers, and making strategic counter-proposals.

How to Play

  • Complete 4 rounds of negotiations
  • Watch the timer bar at the top
  • Use the dropdown menus to set contract terms
  • Make decisions using three main buttons:
    • "Propose Terms" - Submit your contract terms
    • "Make Counter-Offer" - Respond to opponent's proposals
    • "Analyze Contract" - Check current terms for risks

Scoring System

  • +30 points: Milestone-based payment terms
  • +20 points: Fair 50/50 payment splits
  • +15-25 points: Successful counter-offers
  • +10 points: Thorough contract analysis
  • -10 points: High-risk terms

Tips for Success

  • Review both company profiles carefully
  • Balance quick decisions with careful analysis
  • Watch the timer - don't let it run out
  • Aim for terms that benefit both parties
