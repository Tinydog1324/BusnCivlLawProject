How to Play - Regulatory Compliance Race

Game Objective

Navigate through different business departments while maintaining regulatory compliance and avoiding violations.

Gameplay

  • Click "Roll Dice" to move between departments randomly
  • Face compliance challenges in each department
  • Choose the correct regulatory response to earn points
  • Wrong decisions result in violations
  • Game ends after 3 violations

Features

  • Legal Consultation: Use twice per game to get hints
  • Scoring: Earn 100 points for each correct decision
  • Departments: HR, Finance, Operations, Marketing, International Trade

Winning

Aim for the highest score by making correct compliance decisions. Avoid getting 3 violations!
