How to Play - Legal Ethics Dilemma Maze

Game Overview

Navigate through challenging business ethics scenarios and make decisions that will impact your company's future. Your choices matter - each decision affects four key metrics that determine your success.

Key Metrics

  • Company Reputation: Reflects public and industry perception
  • Legal Standing: Indicates compliance with laws and regulations
  • Financial Position: Shows monetary impact of decisions
  • Future Opportunities: Represents potential for growth and partnerships

How to Play

  1. Read each scenario carefully
  2. Consider the potential consequences of each choice
  3. Select your decision by clicking one of the option buttons
  4. Watch how your metrics change based on your choice
  5. Progress through all scenarios to complete the game

Winning the Game

Success is measured by maintaining balanced metrics throughout the game. Try to keep all metrics above 70 for the best outcome. If any metric falls below 40, you're in the danger zone!

Tips

  • Consider long-term impacts of your decisions
  • Balance ethical considerations with business needs
  • Monitor all metrics - they're interconnected
  • Sometimes the most profitable choice isn't the best choice
