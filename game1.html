How to Play - Business Civil Law Challenge

Game Objective

Win the case by reaching 100 points while analyzing real-world business law scenarios!

Game Rules

  • Read each legal case carefully
  • Select the correct legal concept that applies to the case
  • Correct answers earn you 10 points
  • You have 3 lives (attempts) to reach 100 points
  • Incorrect answers cost you 1 life
  • Game ends when you either:
    • Win by reaching 100 points
    • Lose by running out of lives

Tips

  • Pay attention to key details in each case
  • Consider all possible legal concepts before making your choice
  • Use your lives wisely - you only get three!
